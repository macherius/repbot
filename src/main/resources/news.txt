# RepBot news 

New! The 'list' command now shows saved games data. 'tell RepBot help list' for more information.
RepBot has a new command: alert. 'tell RepBot help alert' for the scoop.
See what your friends think of a player! 'tell RepBot help friends' will show you how.
If you find RepBot useful, tell your friends and opponents about it! The more players use RepBot, the more useful RepBot becomes.
RepBot now offers active notification via message for complain, vouch and withdraw events for your account. They are enabled with "tell repbot alert on".
